Wirefox Remote

Wirefox Remote is a simple, elegant and customizable remote control for your desktop. Customize it based on your network speed or make use of its many features.

“Sit back, Relax and Enjoy”

Downloads
  • Configurability

    Specify maximum delay or turn on the option for additional mouse smoothening.

  • Privacy

    No data is ever collected. Whatever happens on your network stays on your network.

  • Security

    We offer the ability to encrypt all remote control traffic between devices by setting a password.

  • History

    History of all attempts to gain remote access to your device is available at all times.

  • Linux support

    Native linux support is available out of the box.
